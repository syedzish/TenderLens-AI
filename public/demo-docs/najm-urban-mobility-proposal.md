# Fictional Proposal: Najm Urban Mobility Smart Parking Response

Respondent: Najm Urban Mobility Systems  
Tender reference: RMMO-SP-2026-014

## Executive Summary

Najm Urban Mobility proposes a hosted smart parking operations platform with enforcement workflows, resident permit services, payment reconciliation, and live occupancy analytics for the five pilot districts.

## Commercial Submission

Bid security: 2% of contract value, valid for 90 days from submission. Najm can extend the validity period if requested during clarification.

## Product and Language Coverage

The operator portal and resident notifications are available in Arabic and English. The enforcement officer mobile interface supports Arabic labels, English labels, and right-to-left layout.

## Availability and Support

The hosted service is designed for 99.5% monthly availability. Critical incident response is staffed 24/7 with a target human acknowledgement within 30 minutes. Standard support tickets receive response within 4 business hours Sunday through Thursday.

## Security and Data Residency

Najm maintains an ISO 27001-aligned information security management system and completed an independent penetration test in March 2026. Production resident data will be hosted in a Riyadh cloud region under a KSA data residency commitment.

## Delivery Plan

District configuration begins after contract award. Field integration and officer training run in parallel. Go-live is planned for 15 October 2026 after staged district acceptance.

## Integrations

The platform integrates with municipal payment gateways through REST callbacks and signed reconciliation exports. Najm exposes REST APIs for occupancy, permits, violation status, and payment events.

## Training

Najm includes role-based training for 40 municipal users across operations, finance reconciliation, enforcement supervisors, and system administrators. Additional training seats can be priced separately.
